  • Full Stack Unpaid Summer Intern

    We are currently looking for some amazing and motivated full-stack unpaid summer interns. We're looking for applicants who are passionate about both architectural and design aspects of developing.

    As a Full-Stack Summer Intern, you will:

    - Work closely with the founders

    - Develop and implement Tolo API endpoints

    - Design and build next generation data analytics dashboard

    - Build custom tools for customer interface

    - Build and maintain design of customer facing website

    - Work with graphics to design and wireframe modern interfaces

    We’re looking for:

    - Experience with building data API’s Familiarity with Ajax design concepts

    - 2+ years experience with any frontend javascript MVC (example: Backbone or AngularJS)

    - Expert level experience with HTML5/Javascript/CSS

    - Familiarity with design concepts

    - Experience with data visualization

    - 2+ years in web/backend development

    What's in it for you:

    - Be a part of an early stage startup with incredible growth opportunities.

    - Meaningful equity.

    - Unlimited vacation and flexible working hours.

    - Familiarity with design concepts
